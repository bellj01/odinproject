Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

A delicious peanut butter and jelly sandwich.

This is a great and perfect meal. You will need:

  • Two slices of bread
  • Jar of peanut butter
  • Jar of jelly
  1. Get two slices of bread
  2. Spread the peanut butter on one slice
  3. Spread the jelly on the other slice
  4. Stick the spreaded sides together
